How to Play

Objective

The objective of the game is to escape the dark by getting to the end of the mazes as quickly as possible.

Gameplay

Prepare to enter a treacherous maze where darkness lurks at every turn. With only a glimmer of light and dwindling health, you must fight your way to the exit, all while facing relentless enemies, deadly traps, and the constant threat of the Skull of Fury hot on your heels. But it's not all doom and gloom—hidden throughout the maze are chests filled with priceless treasures and powerful potions to aid your survival. Use your loot to stock up on potions or upgrade your character, each of whom comes with unique stats and abilities. Choose your hero wisely, for every decision counts. The faster you escape each level, the greater the rewards—so run and conquer before the maze consumes you!

Controls

- Player can move using the arrow keys

- Player can move using the WASD keys

- Player can open their inventory by pressing the shift key

- Player can activate an ability by pressing the E key

- Player can open the shop by pressing the T key

- Player can open the cheat menu by pressing the C key

- Player can stun the Skull of Fury if in range by pressing the Q key

Player Utility

Game Screen

The small skulls indicate the player's current health — each one a symbol of your remaining vitality.


The bloodied skull tracks your ability cool down. It's your cue for unleashing power.


When it glows bright red, your ability is ready to be unleashed — whether it's a devastating stun or a unique special move.

Once used, the skull fades to grey, gradually pulsing back to crimson as your power recharges.

Inventory

Tap the Shift key to open your inventory — your gateway to glory.


Inside, you'll find everything you’ve collected on your journey. Use potions to boost your stats with ease.


Click a potion to instantly drink it, unleashing its effects and enhancing your stats on the fly.

Curious about what a potion does? Hover over it to reveal its hidden powers.


At a glance, you can also see your current stats and the maximum level your character can reach — a glimpse at your true potential.

Tavern

Press T to step into the Tavern — your sanctuary amid the shadows.


Within these walls lies everything you need to survive the darkness beyond.


Need more power? Spend your gold on upgrades and push your stats to new heights.


Running low on potions? Restock your supply and stay battle-ready.


Gold reserves running dry? Trade spare potions for coin and keep your adventure funded.


Cheat Menu

Backed into a corner? Press C to unlock the Cheat Menu — your last resort when all else fails.


Need gold? One click floods your pockets with limitless riches.


Traps giving you trouble? Activate invincibility and walk through danger untouched.


Ability cool downs too slow? Make them instant and unleash your power at will.


Feeling sluggish? Boost your speed and leave your enemies in the dust.


Lost in the shadows? Enable night vision and pierce the darkness with ease.
